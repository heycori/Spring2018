Quality: About This Data Set

This data was determined through statistical analyses such as inter-factor alpha and factor correlations to be a reliable and valid measure of the psychological concepts being studied.

Is the data complete?

Not all participants filled in values for each column. For instance, only n=264 participants answered the questions in the first three pages of the study, because they were added midway through data collection. This is expected. Of more concern are the individuals who left Q14 blank or answered in nonsensical ways i.e. GOOD - the question asked what other factors other than those mentioned in the survey affect their openness to using security measures, with the directive to write "None" if none came to mind.

Is the data coherent?

The data make sense relative to itself. I have performed a number of complicated and simple tests. For instance, I have examined the distributions of values by visually inspecting histograms with the normal curve plotted. I have visually inspected scatterplots to see if expected correlations were discernable; they were.

Is the data correct?

I removed the personally identifiable information before uploading the dataset, but beforehand, I checked for instance that the completion codes recorded in the CSV file matched the completion codes reported on Mechanical Turk. I also looked at patterns of data to see if there were any noticeabe patterns such as a respondent only answering "4, 4, 4, 4," across a row or "1, 2, 3, 4, 1, 2, 3, 4" to indicate a lack of honest answers, there were none.
